Youth Leadership & Executives

The NTBC Youth Ministry is led by dedicated and committed leaders who serve under the authority of the church, providing guidance, organization, and spiritual direction to the youth.

Our leaders are called to serve with humility, integrity, and faithfulness, setting godly examples for others to follow.

“And whosoever will be chief among you, let him be your servant.”
— Matthew 20:27

Youth Executive Positions

  • Youth President
  • Vice President
  • Secretary
  • Assistant Secretary
  • Financial Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Organizing Secretary

Names, roles, and photographs of executives will be added as they are appointed or elected.

Leadership Responsibilities

  • Spiritual guidance and mentorship
  • Planning and organizing youth programs
  • Promoting unity and discipline among members
  • Serving as role models in character and conduct
