Youth Leadership & Executives

Dedicated Servants Leading by Example

The NTBC Youth Ministry is led by dedicated and committed leaders who serve under the authority of the church, providing guidance, organization, and spiritual direction to the youth.

Our leaders are called to serve with humility, integrity, and faithfulness, setting godly examples for others to follow.
