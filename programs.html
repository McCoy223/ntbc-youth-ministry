Youth Programs & Activities

The NTBC Youth Ministry organizes spiritual and developmental programs aimed at strengthening faith, building character, and encouraging fellowship among the youth.

Regular Programs

  • Youth Fellowship Services
  • Bible Studies and Teaching Sessions
  • Prayer Meetings
  • Leadership Training

Special Programs

  • Youth Seminars and Workshops
  • Evangelism and Outreach Programs
  • Youth Conferences and Camps
  • Community Service and Welfare Activities

Purpose of Our Programs

All our programs are designed to help young people grow spiritually, develop godly character, and live out their faith boldly.
